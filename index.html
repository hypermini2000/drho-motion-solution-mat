About DR-HO'S Motion Solution Mat

The DR-HO'S Motion Solution Mat is a passive motion stretch and mobilization device developed by Dr. Michael Ho, a doctor with over 30 years of clinical expertise in hands-on stretching and mobilization techniques. It performs four types of spinal movement: rotation, elongation, extension, and flexion.
